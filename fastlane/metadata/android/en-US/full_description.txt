Mojiii 3 is a free and open source match-3 puzzle game inspired by classic gem-swapping puzzlers.

Swap adjacent emoji gems to create matches of 3 or more in a row or column. Trigger cascading combos, unlock special gems, and progress through increasingly challenging levels.

Features:
- 6 colorful gem types with swappable emoji themes
- Special gems: striped (clears row/col), bomb (3x3 blast), rainbow (clears a color)
- Cascade combos with score multipliers
- Procedurally generated levels with move and time constraints
- Obstacles: ice, stone, chains, and slime
- Boss levels with unique mechanics
- Free/endless mode for casual play
- Power-ups and in-game shop (no real money, coins earned by playing)
- Hint system and deadlock detection
- Background music and sound effects
- Fully offline - no tracking, no ads, no internet required

Mojiii 3 respects your privacy. It collects no data, contains no ads, and requires no permissions.
